Learn Chinese in Taiwan

October 8, 2016

Hi, glad to know that you choose Taiwan to learn Chinese! You can learn Chinese in Taiwan or China. For most foreigners, China is the first choice. I am not surprised. In China, there is a big market for making money, establishing new startups etc.

So why do you choose Taiwan? First, you can learn "Traditional Chinese" in Taiwan. Okay, I know for some learners they do not care whether Traditional, Simplified Chinese or not. If you really love Chinese language, traditional characters, you must come to Taiwan. Second, here there are many tour destinations tourists would like to visit, such as Taipei 101, temples, museums, night markets, mountains, beaches and so forth. Taiwan is worth visiting. Third, food. You just type "40 Taiwanese foods we can't live without" on Google. Fourth, people. Taiwanese are very friendly. we are glad to help if you have questions. Oh lots Taiwanese understand English. If you do not know how to make a complete Chinese sentence, speaking English is okay.

Come on, I look forward to hearing from you! Thank you.
