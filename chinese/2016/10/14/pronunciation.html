Pronunciation

October 14, 2016

Pronunciation is not easy for learners at first. But do not worry, practice makes perfect! Chinese is a tone language! We have 5 tones you should learn:


First tone - high level (long, sustained sound)
Second tone ˊ high rising (start from the lower, then rise)
Third tone ˇ falling and rising (low, then rise)
Fourth tone ˋ falling (from high to low)
Neutral tone light, short

Well, still vague right? I have found an interesting and useful website for you!! It provides the pronunciation of Chinese syllables. You can also choose male, female sound. I highly recommend listening on your computer! Please note that your computer needs "Adobe Flash Player".
